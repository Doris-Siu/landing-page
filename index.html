About me...

“While extroverts are verbal processors, who speak as they think, introverts need to think before we speak.”

My name is Mei Yuk, people usually just call me by my English name, Doris. I come from Hong Kong and I can speak English, Cantonese and Mandarin. I just settled in the United Kingdom in 2021 and there is still so much to explore… Planning to get a driving license here and trying to change my career.

I am an introvert who is responsible, detail-minded and empathetic. I enjoy the time of being alone because it can always give me space to think about a lot of things and keep a state of peace. In my leisure time, I like doing Ringfit, watching Netflix and Youtube.

I enjoy the time being with friends too, particularly playing board games together like Werewolves and Avalon, which are so much fun. Sometimes, we love to have binno dinner together in the cold weather and playing Mahjong!

I like running, hiking, and singing… I love pink (but not every pink color), sea, bubble tea, potatoes, animals…

View of tech…

“Technology makes what was once impossible possible. The design makes it real.”

Technology is indispensable to our daily lives, it exists everywhere. It makes people’s lives much easier and convenient, even though we do not need to understand the operation and mechanism behind, we just enjoy its advantages.

I never thought about getting into this area because it seems too complicated and I did not think I could handle it. However, when I first tried some coding myself, I found out that actually it is not that “unapproachable”, it is hard yet rewarding, just like playing puzzles.

It makes me think about exploring more of it and getting a career in it which I would probably gain great satisfaction doing the job.
